# Rule 6: Verbs

Esperanto verbs do not change for person or number. They have six main endings:
- **-as** (present) → Mi amas (I love)
- **-is** (past) → Mi amis (I loved)
- **-os** (future) → Mi amos (I will love)
- **-us** (conditional) → Mi amus (I would love)
- **-u** (command/subjunctive) → Amu min! (Love me!)
- **-i** (infinitive) → Ami (to love)

## Example Sentences:
- **Mi amas vin.** → I love you.
- **Mi amis vin.** → I loved you.
- **Mi amos vin.** → I will love you.
