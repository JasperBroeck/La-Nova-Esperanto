# Rule 5: Pronouns

Personal pronouns in Esperanto are:
- **mi** (I)
- **vi** (you)
- **li** (he)
- **ŝi** (she)
- **ĝi** (it)
- **si** (oneself)
- **ni** (we)
- **ili** (they)
- **oni** (one, people in general)

Possessive pronouns are formed by adding "-a":
- **mia** (my/mine), **via** (your/yours), **lia** (his), etc.

## Example Sentences:
- **Mi amas vin.** → I love you.
- **Mia hundo amas vian katon.** → My dog loves your cat.
- **Oni diras, ke li amas ŝin.** → It is said that he loves her.
