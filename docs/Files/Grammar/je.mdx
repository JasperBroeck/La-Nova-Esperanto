# Rule 14: The Multipurpose Preposition "je"

Esperanto prepositions have clear meanings, but when no specific preposition applies, **je** is used.

### Examples:
- **Li vetas je la ĉevaloj.** → He bets on the horses.
- **Mi alvenos je la oka horo.** → I will arrive at eight o’clock.
- **Li vizitos nin je lundo.** → He will visit us on Monday.

Alternatively, the **-n** accusative ending can replace "je":
- **Li lundon vizitos nin.** → He will visit us on Monday.
