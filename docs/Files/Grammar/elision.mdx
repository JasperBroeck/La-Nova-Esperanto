# Rule 16: Elision

In Esperanto, the final vowel of a noun or the article **la** may be omitted and replaced by an apostrophe (’), often for poetic or stylistic reasons.

### Examples:
- **L' espero, l' obstino kaj la pacienco…** → Hope, stubbornness, and patience…
- **Ho, mia kor', ne batu maltrankvile…** → Oh, my heart, do not beat nervously…

### Important Notes:
- The final vowel can only be elided if the word does not have a plural (-j) or accusative (-n) ending.
