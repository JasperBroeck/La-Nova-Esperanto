# Rule 10: Accent

In Esperanto, the accent **always** falls on the next-to-last syllable (penultimate syllable) of a word.

### Examples:
- **amiko** (friend) → **a-MI-ko**
- **telefono** (telephone) → **te-le-FO-no**
- **Esperanto** → **es-pe-RAN-to**

Even when a noun's final vowel "-o" is dropped for poetic reasons, the stress remains where it originally was:
- **l' espero** (the hope) → **es-PE-ro**
